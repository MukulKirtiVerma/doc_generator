About DocGen

We're on a mission to make document conversion simple, accurate, and accessible for everyone.

Our Story

DocGen was born out of frustration with existing document conversion tools that didn't preserve formatting or required complex software installations.

Our founding team of AI engineers and document specialists came together in 2024 with a simple goal: create an intelligent, cloud-based solution that could transform images into perfectly formatted documents with just a few clicks.

By leveraging Anthropic's Claude AI model, we've developed a platform that understands document structure and can recreate it in popular formats. What used to take hours of manual work can now be done in seconds.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses by making document digitization intelligent, efficient, and accessible.
